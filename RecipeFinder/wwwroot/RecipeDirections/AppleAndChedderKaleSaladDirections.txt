Combine greens, apple slices, cheese and pecans in a large bowl.

Dressing
Combine shallot, lemon juice, apple cider vinigar, honey and olive oil in a small bowl.
Whisk the mixture until well combined.
Season with salt and pepper, to tast.
Pour dressing over salad and toss gently to combine.
Serve immediately.
Refrigerate any leftovers.
