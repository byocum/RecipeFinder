In a large bowl combine egg, bread crumbs, 1/2 cup spaghetti sauce, garlic and rosemary.
Add beef and turkey or sausage.
Mix well.
Divide the meat mixture into six portions and shape each portion into a loaf.
Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 30 min.
Spoon a tablespoon of spaghetti sause over each meat loaf and add shredded cheese on top.
Bake five more min or until center reads 160 degrees with a thermometer.
